Connecting with you

Bonsai is a Japanese art form using trees grown in containers. people's interest is growing in having bonsai plant in their houses.

But, people are busy in their daily life activities in this 21st century. They don't have time to visit shop and buy them. So, we developed this website to make the buying and selling of Bonsai Plants possible through online. With this we are connecting ourself with you through Bonsai.

Who are we

We are the student of IIC working on a project to create an e-commerce websites.

Our Objective

With the increase of modernization people are getting more busy. they don't have time for visiting market to buy something they want. so, our objective to develop this website is to help people find their desired bonsai plants in a single place.

Our Vision

As, the use of network is increasing and online shopping is also in increasing form. we have the vision to make this website the 1st destination of everyone who wants to buys bonsai plants.
